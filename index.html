Project: Cable-driven upper body exoskeleton (2021-22)

This was a project for my thesis of Master's degree (M. Tech). Aim was to desgin and simulate an upper body Exoskeleton to provide assisstance at elbow joint during lift and hold action by a human. A kinmeatic model of the human body was derived and simulated in Simulink and Adams with the experimental data, which was a lift, walk and drop expreiment of 100 men. Data contains, position, velocity, acceleration and torque. Develpoed human model was based on the proprtion of human body based on various itrature survey. A cable driven mechanishm was develped in Solidworks and its mathematical model was formed. It was imported in MATLAB-Simulink along with upper body of the develped human model. Maximum and minimum posotion/velocity contraints on each joint of Exoskelton was applied. Later stage it was simulated for position control of elbow joint using PID and PI.

Project: Path planning using D* Algorithm (2021)

Implementation of D* algorithm in MATLAB code. It generates a new local path in the workspace wherever changes occur. Changes include removal or addition of obstacle. Velocity of the obstacle was not considered, only generation of path from 8 grid method.

Project: Six Degree of Freedom arm Manipulator (2017)

Built a 6 DOF Robot manipulator for minor project in Bachelor’s degree to demonstrate forward kinematics of a manipulator. At joints DC motors were used and controlled using potentiometer and Arduino Uno. Potentiometer was used to provide joint angle, externally. 2-Finger End Effector was designed in AutoCAD and built using steel plates and rubber for the grip. End effector was actuated using Servo motors.

Competition: Swadeshi Microprocessor Challenge (2020-21)

In space movement of human is extreme difficult and limited due to the complex deign of Extravehicular Mobility Unit (space suit) and constrained movement inside it. To overcome this, astronauts require either a better form of EMU (Extravehicular Mobility Unit) or an automated robot, which can perform some simple task or maybe complex task required outside the space station. This project deals with the later part of the solution. This will be the first step in this area. At first, it will have a very basic task to do, like monitoring our Space Station form outside, documenting the work of astronauts in the space, keeping track of all activities, holding tools for astronauts (wight will be a negligible factor in Space due to microgravity).
