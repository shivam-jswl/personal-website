Cable-driven upper body exoskeleton

Project details

Desgin and simulation of an upper body Exoskeleton to provide assisstance at elbow joint during lift and hold action by a human. A kinmeatic model of the human body was derived and simulated in Simulink and Adams with the experimental data, which was a lift, walk and drop expreiment of 100 men. Data contains, position, velocity, acceleration and torque. Develpoed human model was based on the proprtion of human body based on various itrature survey. A cable driven mechanishm was develped in Solidworks and its mathematical model was formed. It was imported in MATLAB-Simulink along with upper body of the develped human model. Maximum and minimum posotion/velocity contraints on each joint of Exoskelton was applied. Later stage it was simulated for position control of elbow joint using PID and PI.
